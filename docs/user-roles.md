# User Roles

All roles observed or inferred from quicklister.co.uk.

---

## Role Overview

| Role | Auth Required | Can List | Can Search | Can Enquire | Can Admin |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Guest / Buyer / Tenant | No | No | Yes | Yes | No |
| Private Seller | Yes | Sales only | Yes | No | No |
| Private Landlord | Yes | Lettings only | Yes | No | No |
| Commercial Owner | Yes | Commercial only | Yes | No | No |
| Pro Lister | Yes | Up to 6/month (all types) | Yes | No | No |
| Platform Admin | Yes | No | Yes | No | Yes |

---

## Guest / Buyer / Tenant

**Who they are:** Any unauthenticated visitor to the site — a prospective buyer, tenant, or someone browsing listings.

**What they can do:**
- Browse the public property search
- View individual listing detail pages
- Submit an enquiry (name, email, phone, message) on a listing
- Book a viewing request

**What they cannot do:**
- Create or manage listings
- Access the platform dashboard
- View other users' messages

**Notes:**
- No account required to submit an enquiry
- Enquiries are routed directly into the property owner's inbox

---

## Private Seller

**Who they are:** A homeowner or property owner listing a residential property for sale without using a traditional estate agent.

**What they can do:**
- Register and create an account
- Purchase a sales package (Saver, Exposure, or Premium)
- Create and manage sales listings
- Upload property photos, floorplans, and documents
- Submit ID and proof of ownership for verification
- Manage buyer enquiries via the platform inbox
- Schedule and track viewings
- Accept an offer and instruct solicitors via the platform
- Purchase AML check and professional photography add-ons
- View performance reports for their listings

**Restrictions:**
- Cannot list rental properties without a separate lettings package
- Cannot list commercial properties without a commercial package

---

## Private Landlord

**Who they are:** A property owner renting out one or a small number of residential properties without using a letting agent.

**What they can do:**
- Register and create an account
- Purchase a lettings package (Saver, Exposure, or Pro Lister subscription)
- Create and manage lettings listings
- Upload photos, certificates (gas safety, EPC, electrical), and ownership docs
- Manage tenant enquiries via the platform inbox
- Schedule viewings independently
- Order tenant reference checks via Goodlord (£25/applicant)
- Pause and re-list a property if a reference fails
- View performance reports

**Restrictions:**
- Professional letting agents are explicitly prohibited from using the platform
- Cannot list properties they do not own

---

## Commercial Owner

**Who they are:** An owner of a commercial property (office, retail unit, light industrial, or land) seeking to let or sell without a commercial agent.

**What they can do:**
- Purchase a commercial package (Commercial Let or Commercial Sale)
- Create and manage commercial listings
- Manage enquiries and viewings
- Access Zoopla Commercial syndication

**Restrictions:**
- Same no-agent rule applies — must be the property owner

---

## Pro Lister

**Who they are:** A high-volume private landlord managing multiple properties who wants a subscription-based plan rather than per-listing fees.

**What they can do:**
- Everything a Private Landlord can do
- List up to 6 properties per month under the plan
- All listings syndicated across all four portals (Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket, PrimeLocation)
- Choose between monthly (£42/month, 3-month minimum) or annual (£403/year) billing

**Restrictions:**
- Still must be the property owner — not a professional agent
- Minimum 3-month commitment on monthly plan

---

## Platform Admin (Quicklister Staff)

**Who they are:** Internal Quicklister employees who manage the platform and verify listings.

**What they can do:**
- Review submitted listings and ownership/ID documents
- Approve or reject listings (with reason)
- Communicate with users regarding verification issues
- View all users and properties on the platform
- Manage portal syndication
- Access support queue

**What they cannot do:**
- List properties themselves (conflict of interest with the platform's no-agent rule)

---

## Explicit Exclusions (Per Platform Rules)

> "Professional agents cannot advertise on the platform."

The platform is strictly for **property owners only**. Estate agents, letting agents, and property management companies are prohibited from creating listings, regardless of whether they hold a power of attorney or similar authority.
